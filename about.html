About Us

Vila Orion

VILA ORION

Vila Orioni is located in Saranda city. Just a few minutes away from the beach, our Hotel features units with air conditioning and free WiFi. Perfect for families our units also offer a small kitchen and living room, where you can spend the day.

Vila Orion Welcomes Tourists from all over the world. Ereikoússa is 35 km from the apartment, while Ksamil is 12 km from the property.

Some of the units have a satellite TV, a fully equipped kitchen with a fridge, and a private bathroom with a shower and a hairdryer.

Sarande Main Beach is 1.1 km from the apartment, while Maestral Beach is 1.8 km from the property.
